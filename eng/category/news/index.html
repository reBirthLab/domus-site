Car Sales Centre opened on Kirova Avenue

Date: 28 March, 2013

Car Sales Centre “Protect” is opened on Kirova Avenue, Dnipropetrovsk!

The idea of building was designed by Rodion Tovstik in 2003. The structure has an impressive and original architecture despite the fact its project has been changed and corrected a lot during realisation and as a result, many interesting details are lost. Nevertheless, we are really glad that construction of the building is finally completed and our city has got one more piece of contemporary architecture!
